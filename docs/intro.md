# Welcome to ELVIRA

**ELVIRA** is an open publication catalog, web portal and PDF viewer built
around the **OPDS** family of specifications. Developed at
[FIIT STU](https://www.fiit.stuba.sk/) in Bratislava. In production at
FIIT STU and [KU Ružomberok](https://www.ku.sk/).

These docs are deliberately short. They&rsquo;re the on-ramp, not the
encyclopedia. For everything else, the
[wiki](https://github.com/EvilFlowersCatalog/EvilFlowersCatalog/wiki) and
the [auto-generated OpenAPI](https://elvira.digital/EvilFlowersCatalog/)
are authoritative.
